This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled when you use the Fine Airplay Recevier application (the "App") developed by Fine Grain Studio ("we," "us," or "our").

By using the App, you agree to the handling of information in accordance with this policy.

If you do not agree with any part of this Privacy Policy, please uninstall the App immediately.

Information Collection and Use

We are committed to minimizing data handling. The App does not require you to provide personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use its core features.

We do not collect or upload your AirPlay content (audio/video/photos) to our servers.

Local Network / AirPlay Content Handling

The App functions as an AirPlay receiver and processes data transmitted from your devices over your local network to provide playback/receiving functionality. We do not access, view, store, or upload your AirPlay content to our servers. Any temporary buffering required for playback is handled locally on your device.

How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal information for core functionality, our direct use of information is limited. When applicable, information may be used to provide and maintain the App, enable purchases or subscriptions (processed by Microsoft), display advertising content (served by third-party ad providers), and comply with legal obligations when required.

Infatica P2B Network Participation

By using this app you will become a peer on the Infatica P2B network. This means that a tiny bit of your idle Internet bandwidth resources can be shared with this network. The P2B network spreads its traffic through millions of idle peers and therefore has minimal effect on total bandwidth consumption.

Please note that NONE of your personal information is accessed and NO USER DATA is collected or shared with external parties except for the IP location data.

Data Storage

The App is designed to operate locally. We do not maintain user accounts and do not store your AirPlay content on our servers. If the App stores settings or preferences, they are stored locally on your device.
